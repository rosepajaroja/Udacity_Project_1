Hello there!

Welcome to my blog! My name is Rose, and I am a Happiness Engineer for Automattic, the company behind WordPress.com. I'm also a Computer Science student at the University of London, and I'm passionate about web development and design.

I created this blog as a project for Udacity's Frontend Development Nanodegree. I was fortunate enough to receive a seat in this program through the "Next Generation Tech Booster Scholarship," which has allowed me to further my education and develop my skills in web development.

When I'm not working or studying, I enjoy spending time with my family. I'm a proud wife and mother of three boys, and we share our home with five furry friends. Cooking and gardening are two of my favorite hobbies, and I love to experiment with new recipes and grow my own fruits and vegetables.

Through this blog, I hope to share my knowledge and experiences in web development and design. I'll be posting about a variety of topics, including WordPress tips and tricks, web development techniques, and design trends. I'll also share some of my favorite recipes and gardening tips.

Thank you for visiting my blog, and I hope you find the information here useful and inspiring. Don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or feedback. Let's connect and learn together!
